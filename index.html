Why should you maintain a healthy routine?

Maintaining a healthy routine helps improve overall health and wellness, and can reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes and high blood pressure. It also helps to improve mood and sense of self-satisfaction.

How to maintain a healthy routine

  • Eat healthy and balanced meals that include all food groups.
  • Drink enough water to maintain hydration.
  • Do regular exercise to strengthen the digestive and cardiovascular systems.
  • Get enough sleep to feel refreshed and energized.
  • Avoid smoking, alcohol and drugs.
